Who we are

Web3Ladies2.0 is the #1 sought-after Web3 community in Africa empowering a generation of female industry leaders who will build, nurture, and develop a sustainable Web3 space.

Our aim is to foster a community of excellent, passionate, and groundbreaking ladies in the African Web3 space, who can work together, not only to challenge the status quo but also to create impactful open-sourced solutions in the Web3 space.
